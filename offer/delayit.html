FAQ's

What is the minimum amount I can extend?

The minimum amount is 500₹.

How is the fee calculated?

The fee is calculated as 4.2% of the amount and number of days.

Can I apply for multiple extensions?

Yes, multiple extensions are possible, subject to approval, with each having its own fee.

If I have today return date can I extend today?

No, you can't extend if today is your return date, you can only extend before the return date.

No. of days I can extend?

You can extend your amount between 15–30 days.

I can't extend my return date more than 30 days?

Yes, you can extend more than 30 days but it depends upon your membership type.
