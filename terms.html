LendLink Terms and Conditions

Eligibility

LendLink requires all Barrowers to be at least 18 years of age or older.

Use these funds only in emergencies. Linklend provides help only to those who are in urgent need.

User Responsibilities

To provide you with the emergency funds, we need to collect the required amount first. It will take a few hours to arrange the funds, so we kindly ask for your patience during this time to get your funds.

You need to pay the PIBT (Pay Interest Before Taking) before borrowing money from Lendlink.

Interest details

The interest for your amount will be displayed in your account.

It will be automatically calculated and added daily based on the amount you borrowed.

The interest will continue to add until you repay the full amount that you Barrowed.
