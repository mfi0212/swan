Eligibility

LendLink requires all Barrowers to be at least 18 years of age or older.

Use these funds only in emergencies. Linklend provides help only to those who are in urgent need.

User Responsibilities

As a LendLink user, it's important to make sure you pay the interest on your loan as agreed and repay the full borrowed amount by the due date. This helps maintain a good record and ensures smooth transactions for future loans.

You need to pay the BTF (Before Taking Funds) fee when borrowing money from Lendlink.
